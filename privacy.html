Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Whop Clips ("the App") collects only the minimum information necessary to function, including TikTok account credentials (access tokens) used solely to post content on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your TikTok access token is used exclusively to post video content to your TikTok account as directed by you. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Credentials are stored locally on your own device in a .env configuration file. We do not store your credentials on any external servers.

4. TikTok Data

This App uses the TikTok Content Posting API. Data accessed through TikTok's API is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy available at tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Third Party Services

The App may interact with third-party services including TikTok, YouTube, and Kick. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the App and its configuration files from your device. To revoke TikTok access, go to TikTok Settings → Security → Authorized Apps and remove Whop Clips.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through our GitHub page.
